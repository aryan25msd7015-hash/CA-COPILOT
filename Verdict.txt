**Verdict**

**Yes, this can be an MVP and CA firms can pay for it.** However, it is currently best positioned as a **founder-assisted paid pilot**, not yet as a self-service replacement for every tool a CA firm uses.

### Why Firms May Pay

CA firms already pay for practice software:

- [Practive](https://www.practive.in/pricing/) charges approximately `₹75–₹99/user/month`.
- [Jamku](https://madrecha.com/jamku/pricing/) starts around `₹6,500/year`.
- [CA Manage](https://www.camanage.in/) offers a `₹2,999/month` professional plan.
- [CAGos](https://cagos.io/) lists plans from `₹999–₹7,999/month`.
- Newer AI-focused products such as [CaSync](https://casync.in/) start around `₹2,999/month`.

This proves there is willingness to pay, but the market is price-sensitive and already crowded.

**Your strongest differentiation is not basic client or task management.** It is producing billable, review-ready outputs:

1. MSME Section 43B(h) audit and Clause 22 report
2. Drawing Power statements
3. CA certificates
4. Client-level compliance risk calendar
5. Actual-hours versus profitability analysis

These modules can directly save staff time or generate revenue.

**Best Initial Customer**

Target firms with:

- `5–50` employees
- `50–300` business clients
- Heavy GST, tax audit, bank finance, and certificate work
- Existing workflows across Tally, Excel, email, and WhatsApp

Solo CAs and very small firms will likely prefer cheaper tools.

**Recommended MVP Scope**

Launch with only this bundle:

- Advanced compliance calendar and reminders
- MSME 43B(h) audit
- Drawing Power statements
- CA certificate generation
- Excel/Tally imports and mandatory partner review

Keep Lease Intelligence, RFP, MCA documents, and timesheet auditing as premium add-ons until usage is proven.

**Before Charging Firms**

The current product needs these commercial-readiness improvements:

- Replace JSON text boxes with guided Excel/Tally/PDF imports
- Add approval, version history, evidence links, and reviewer sign-off
- Benchmark accuracy using real anonymized client files
- Provide Indian data hosting, encryption, backups, retention controls, and security documentation
- Add onboarding/import assistance and reliable error handling
- Clearly label generated documents as drafts requiring CA verification

**Suggested Pricing Test**

Start with 5 paid design-partner firms:

- `₹5,000–₹10,000` implementation fee
- `₹3,000–₹6,000/month` for up to 50 clients
- `₹8,000–₹15,000/month` for larger firms

Do not offer the pilot completely free. Even a small payment is necessary to prove real willingness to pay.

**Bottom Line**

The product is **strong enough for an MVP paid pilot**. Firms will pay when it reliably completes a painful workflow and saves measurable hours. They will not pay merely because it contains many modules.

Position it as:

> “Generate reviewed compliance and banking deliverables in minutes, using your existing Tally and Excel data.”

Not as another all-in-one CA office management platform.
